# Strategic Partnerships: Initial Concepts

**Owner:** Strategic Partnerships Manager
**Date:** 2025-12-09

## 1. Insurance Carriers ("TrueManifest for Risk")

### The Pitch
"Stop guessing the value of what you insure. MyARK provides real-time, AI-verified valuation and visual proof of condition, reducing claim friction and fraud."

### Value Prop
- **Risk Reduction:** Visual Truth reduces "pre-existing damage" claims.
- **Premium Optimization:** Real-time portoflio values allow dynamic premium adjustment.
- **Customer Delight:** "Digital Locker" features improve policyholder retention.

### Integration Approach
- **Deep-Link:** "Add to Policy" button in their app opens MyARK.
- **Data Sync:** Daily webhook sync of "Total Insured Value".

---

## 2. Estate Planners & Attorneys ("MyARK Legacy")

### The Pitch
"The modern way to settle estates. Turn a physical house full of 'stuff' into a digital ledger that can be equitably divided or auctioned in clicks."

### Value Prop
- **Speed:** Inventory a home 10x faster with AI visual scanning.
- **Fairness:** AI valuations prevent family disputes over "what's it worth".
- **Liquidity:** One-click "Send to Auction" for unwanted items.

---

## 3. Restoration & Moving Companies ("Chain of Custody")

### The Pitch
"The ultimate proof of condition. Sean items before you move them, and prove they arrived safely."

### Value Prop
- **Liability Protection:** Time-stamped, geolocated, AI-verified photos before/after handling.
- **Trust:** Offer "MyARK Certified Move" as a premium service.
