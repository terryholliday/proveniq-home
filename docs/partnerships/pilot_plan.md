# Enterprise Pilot Implementation Plan

**Objective:** Onboard 3 Pilot Partners by Q3 2025.
**Success Criteria:** 500 active shared users, NPS > 50.

## 1. Targets
1.  **Carrier A (The Giant):** Focus on High Net Worth homeowners.
2.  **Restoration Co B (The Vendor):** Focus on post-disaster inventory speed.
3.  **Marketplace C (The Reseller):** Focus on authentication (Visual Truth).

## 2. Timeline
*   **Week 1:** Sign LOIs.
*   **Week 2:** Tenant Provisioning (API Keys, Branding Config).
*   **Week 3:** "Train the Trainer" sessions with Partner Agents.
*   **Week 4:** Go Live (Soft Launch with 50 users).

## 3. Success Metrics (KPIs)
*   **Time to Inventory:** Reduced from 4 hours -> 30 mins?
*   **Claim Accuracy:** Reduction in "supplement" requests?
*   **User Adoption:** % of policyholders accepting the digital invite.
