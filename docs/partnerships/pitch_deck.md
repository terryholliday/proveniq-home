# MyARK Partnership Pitch Deck

**Target Audience:** Insurance Carriers (P&C), Restoration Vendors, Estate Managers

## Slide 1: The Problem
*   **For Users:** "I don't know what I own until I lose it." (Underinsurance, claim friction).
*   **For Carriers:** "We don't know what we're insuring." (Premium leakage, fraud risk).

## Slide 2: Introducing MyARK
*   **The "Digital Twin" for Physical Assets.**
*   AI-powered inventory capture (photo -> valuation in seconds).
*   "Visual Truth" verification (provenance + condition via immutable ledger).

## Slide 3: The Opportunity (Why Partner?)
*   **For Carriers:**
    *   **Acquisition:** Offer MyARK Premium as a "perk" to win policyholders.
    *   **Risk Reduction:** Real-time visibility into high-value items (Jewelry, Art).
    *   **Stickiness:** Users with a digital inventory are 3x less likely to churn.

## Slide 4: Integration Tiers
1.  **Referral:** Simple affiliate link. "Protect your specialized items with MyARK."
2.  **Data Sync (API):** User consents to share total coverage value for automated policy right-sizing.
3.  **White Label:** "State Farm Home Inventory, powered by MyARK."

## Slide 5: Pilot Proposal
*   **Goal:** Onboard 500 high-net-worth policyholders.
*   **Duration:** 3 Months.
*   **Success Metric:** 20% uptake, < 5% churn.
