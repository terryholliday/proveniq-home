# **App Name**: MyARK

## Core Features:

- AI-Powered Item Identification (The Scanner): Analyzes images to identify items and populate fields like Name, Category, Description, Condition, Purchase Price, and Market Value. Includes vehicle-specific data and shipping estimates.
- Batch & Multi-Image Scanning: Enables efficient item addition by processing multiple images as a single item from different angles or as separate items in a queue.
- Duplicate Detection: Prevents inventory clutter by identifying items with the same name and offering options to add quantity to the existing item or create a new entry.
- AI-Powered Search ("Ask Your Ark"): Converts natural language questions into structured search filters to quickly find items in the inventory.
- Lending Tracker: Tracks items lent to friends or family, including contact information, lent date, and expected return date.
- AI-Powered Move Planner: Organizes moves by creating virtual boxes, assigning destination rooms, and providing AI-generated packing plans with declutter suggestions, logical groupings, and packing priority.
- Printable Smart Labels (QR Codes): Generates printable labels with QR codes for physical moving boxes, allowing quick access to contents via scanning.
- AI Room Audit (Space Mapper): Uses the device camera to compare the visible items in a room against the inventory to identify missing, misplaced, or unexpected items.
- Market Valuation Tool: Provides real-time appraisal by simulating a search of recent auction results and giving an updated Current Value, Market Trend, and list of Comparable Sales.
- AI Sales Ad Generation: Creates sales listings for various platforms by selecting a target audience and tone, generating compelling ad copy.
- ARKive Auctions Platform: Facilitates private auctions by generating auction titles, descriptions, and starting bids, and suggesting logical lots.
- Risk Assessment Simulator: Simulates common household disasters to understand financial exposure by analyzing item locations and value.
- One-Tap Claim Generation: Streamlines insurance claims by compiling relevant data into a submission-ready report for insurance carriers or warranty providers.
- Legacy Planner: Offers a suite for estate planning with beneficiary management, item assignment, legacy stories, secure document vault, and an AI will generator.
- Email Purchase Importer (Simulated): Automatically scans emails for receipts from major retailers, adding found purchases to an inbox for review and import.
- AI-Powered Repair Services: Generates realistic repair cost estimates and provides an option to select from technicians or put the job out for bids.
- AI Image Editing Tools: Improves item photos with AI tools to remove backgrounds and perform smart crops.
- Onboarding & Guided Tours: Greets new users with an animated slideshow and provides interactive guided tours to highlight key features.
- User Guide: Offers a comprehensive, searchable in-app help center with detailed articles on every feature.
- Authentication & Tier Management: Provides a full user login/signup system with a tier structure, and grants full Pro features during an 'Early Access Mode'.
- Data Management: Allows users to download and restore their inventory data as a JSON file, ensuring data control.
- Internal Sales Training Portal: Offers a completely separate, password-protected sub-application designed to train an internal sales team.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep indigo (#4F46E5) to convey trust, sophistication, and technological innovation.
- Background color: Light gray (#F8FAFC) for a clean and modern feel.
- Accent color: Teal (#2DD4FF) for interactive elements and highlights, providing a sense of energy and forward-thinking.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a modern, machined, objective, neutral look.
- Use modern, minimalist icons from 'lucide-react' to represent actions and categories.
- Responsive layout with a fixed sidebar on desktop and a bottom navigation bar on mobile. Prioritize clear information hierarchy.
- Subtle fade-in animations and slide-up transitions to enhance user experience without being distracting.
